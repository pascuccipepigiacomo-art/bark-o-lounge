About Us

A canine social
club with a heart

Tucked in the north end of Vashon Island, Bark o' Lounge is a home-based daycare set on a large, fully secured compound with covered shelter for those classic Pacific Northwest drizzle days.

We share our home with cats, so we look for dogs that are relaxed around other animals. What you'll find here is a calm, supervised environment where dogs play, nap, and feel genuinely at ease.

House Rules

What makes
a good fit

Our small size means the right match matters. Here's what we ask.

🐾
Socialized dogs welcome
Your dog should be comfortable around other dogs and people. We'll have a quick chat before your first booking.
🏠
Housebroken (for boarding)
Weekend boarding guests must be housebroken. Day playcare is more flexible — give us a call.
🐱
Cats live here
Our cats are part of the household. Dogs with a high prey drive toward cats are not a good fit for our home.
🌿
Space to run and rest
Our large secured compound has both open-run space and a covered shelter — ideal for PNW weather days.
⚠️
No high prey drive dogs
Please be honest about your dog's behavior around small animals. Safety is always our first priority.
📞
Always book in advance
We cap at 4 dogs, which means spots fill up. A quick call goes a long way — we love to chat about your pup.
